The folllowing is a recipe for a delicious and easy to make banana bread.

  1. 3 Bananas
  2. 1/2 Cup Melted Butter
  3. 1/2 Cup White Sugar
  4. 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
  5. 2 Eggs
  6. 1/2 Cup of Milk
  7. 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  8. 1&1/2 Cup Flour
  9. 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  10. 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  11. A sprinkle of Cinnamon
  12. Handful of pecans

Baking Directions

Mix all your ingredients in a bowl in the same order they are listed.

Bake the banana bread batter at 355Â°Ffor 55 minutes.

Make sure to let banana bread cool down completely before cutting a slice. Last but not least enjoy!
